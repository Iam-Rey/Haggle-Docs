Integration Guides

Haggle is a platform that allows users to compare prices of products from different e-commerce merchants. Haggle is accessible to both authenticated and unauthenticated users, but with the authenticated users having full access to the platform.

How does Haggle work?

Haggle goes to various merchants/ e-commerce stores and gets aggregated product based on customer reviews and ratings and price, and displays them to you. When you use the search box to search for a product, or select a product through the category section, your request/function will be sent to the database, which then releases the data of the product you searched, or the category you selected, to you. The data from the various available merchants have been scraped using specific scraping tools. See SDKs and Plugins for more info on the scraping tools.

Using Haggle

To access the complete features of haggle, authentication is required for all users. See Authentication for steps on how to get authenticated.

Navigating through Haggle

Haggle has a very friendly user interface which makes navigating through the platform easy. Below are some of the haggle navigation features

  1. You can click on the icon on the top right corner of your screen to access your dashboard. You'll see you registered details on your dashboard. You can also edit and upload a profile photo.
  2. On the navigation bar of everypage, there are options and features from which you can select by just clicking on them, and you'll be redirected to their pages. Some features on the nav bar include the search box, the category section etc
  3. Search - The search bar is found at the top of the page and is used to navigate through products. When you input a keyword into the search bar, haggle runs a filter function, and only displays products with that keyword you searched for or with keywords similar to it.
  4. Category - Haggle has a category section on the top of the page where you can select from the various available categories. The category section eases navigation through the platform as options selected by you are what will be displayed.
  5. Compare Prices - After you select a product, you'll then be taken to the comparison page where you can compare prices of that particular product from different merchants. In this case, an unauthenticated user only gets to view prices of 2 merchants, while an authenticated user gets full access to all prices.
  6. Go to Shop - After comparison, you can decide to go directly to the product page of the selected merchant where you can make purchase. A ref link that leads to the purchase page of a product is available on every product page on haggle. Users must be authenticated to use this feature.
  7. Comments and Share - You can share a product page link directly to your social media handles, or send them to third parties via our email feature. You can also make comments and read other prople's comment in the comment section under each product, to know other users thought about a particular product or service.
  8. To log out, navigate to your dashboard and scroll up, you'll see a log out button. Click it and you will be logged out of haggle
